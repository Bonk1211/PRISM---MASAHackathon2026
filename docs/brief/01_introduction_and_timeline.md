## Foreword

### Hannover Re — Strategic Partner

Hannover Re is proud to partner with the Malaysian Actuarial Student Association (MASA) as Strategic Partner for the MASA Hackathon: R-Ignite 2026. Through initiatives like the MASA Hackathon, we support actuarial students in applying their skills to real-world risk challenges.

Climate risk and sustainability are actively reshaping insurance and reinsurance. They require analytical depth, structured thinking and forward-looking solutions grounded in data. Through this partnership, students work with real-world datasets, explore ESG considerations, and develop innovative approaches to current risk challenges.

For students, events like this offer a valuable opportunity to apply analytical and modelling skills to practical questions while gaining insights into how the insurance and reinsurance industry approaches complex risks.

Hannover Re is proud to support platforms that develop future actuarial talent and strengthen collaboration between academia and industry. We look forward to seeing how the next generation of actuaries will shape the future of risk analysis.

### Lew Xuan Yee — Chairperson, MASA Hackathon 2026: R-Ignite

It is my great pleasure to welcome you to **MASA Hackathon 2026: R-Ignite**. This year's theme reflects the spirit of innovation, creativity, and analytical thinking that drives the evolution of actuarial practice in the digital age. As the world continues to face increasingly complex global challenges, the actuarial profession plays a vital role in developing data-driven insights and sustainable solutions. Through this hackathon, we aim to provide a platform for students to explore how actuarial thinking, technology, and collaboration can be applied to address real-world issues.

The theme **R-Ignite** centres on the growing challenges of climate risk and its impact on the insurance and reinsurance industry. In this hackathon, participants will take on the role of actuarial consultants for a multinational reinsurance firm, exploring how climate-related factors can affect long-term financial resilience. By analysing data from the World Bank's World Development Indicators, participants will examine climate trends, explore their connection to natural disaster insurance claims in Southeast Asia, and apply analytical thinking to propose practical strategies that help organisations better prepare for and manage climate-related risks in the future.

I would like to extend my sincere appreciation to our sponsors and partners for their invaluable support in making MASA Hackathon 2026 possible. We are grateful to our venue partner, ASSA UCSI, and our strategic partner, Hannover Re, for their collaboration and commitment to supporting the development of future actuarial talents. I would also like to thank our organising committee, and judges for their dedication and hard work in bringing this event to life. Finally, I congratulate all participants for embracing this opportunity to challenge themselves and explore innovative solutions. May this hackathon ignite new ideas, foster meaningful collaboration, and inspire the next generation of actuarial professionals to shape a more resilient future.

## Introduction

### Welcome to MASA Hackathon 2026!

MASA is back with its annual hackathon in 2026, themed **"MASA Hackathon 2026: R-Ignite"**.

This highly anticipated two-month event offers Actuarial Science students a unique opportunity to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world problems. With a focus on **R programming**, the hackathon combines workshop and presentation, both held physically at **UCSI University (Kuala Lumpur Campus)**.

Participants will gain hands-on experience, enhance their analytical skills, and network with industry professionals. This event bridges the gap between academia and industry, fostering innovation and preparing participants for future challenges in the actuarial field.
